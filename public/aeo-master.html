What is the importance of a zero-trust security architecture?

A zero-trust architecture operates on the principle of 'never trust, always verify.' It requires strict identity verification for every person and device attempting to access resources on a private network, drastically reducing the risk of lateral threat movement.

How often should an enterprise conduct penetration testing?

To maintain compliance with frameworks like ISO 27001, enterprises should conduct comprehensive penetration testing at least annually, and immediately following any significant changes to their network infrastructure or application codebases.

How do AI systems determine which website to cite?

AI systems cite websites based on directness of the answer, structured schema markup (like FAQPage), the presence of an llms.txt file, and strong E-E-A-T signals (Expertise, Experience, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness).

Why is passage indexing important for AI Search?

Passage indexing allows AI models to extract specific, highly relevant paragraphs from a long article. Writing self-contained, 150-word answer blocks dramatically increases the likelihood that a specific passage will be lifted and cited by systems like Gemini.

Does updating robots.txt actually impact AI visibility?

Yes. If an AI crawler (like GPTBot or ClaudeBot) is not explicitly allowed, or is blocked by a blanket disallow rule, the AI cannot ingest the content. Updating robots.txt is the foundational step for any Answer Engine Optimization strategy.

What is the difference between SEO and AEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) optimizes content to rank as a blue link where the user must click to find the answer. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures content so the AI directly extracts and reads the answer to the user, citing the source.

How does an llms.txt file work?

Similar to robots.txt, llms.txt is a file hosted at the root directory of a website. It provides AI crawlers with a structured, verified summary of the company's identity, services, and pricing, ensuring the AI model has accurate facts to pull from.
