Signal 2 — Schema Markup & Structured Data

Schema markup is machine-readable JSON-LD code that tells AI systems exactly what your website is about. Without schema, AI must guess your business type, services and location — and it frequently guesses wrong, citing competitors instead. The most important schema types for AI visibility are Organization (establishes your entity in Google Knowledge Graph), FAQPage (feeds directly into AI Overview answers), Service (defines what you offer with pricing), HowTo (step-by-step content AI cites in instructional answers), and WebSite with SearchAction. According to MIRAC Technologies analysis, websites with FAQPage schema are four times more likely to appear in Google AI Overviews than pages without it. Our checker identifies every missing schema type and generates the exact JSON-LD code needed.

Signal 3 — llms.txt File

llms.txt is a plain text file placed at yourwebsite.com/llms.txt that provides AI language models with structured, authoritative information about your business. Created as the AI equivalent of robots.txt, it tells ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude and Gemini exactly who your company is, every service you offer with pricing, markets you serve, and how to contact you. Fewer than 500 websites globally have implemented llms.txt, making it one of the highest-ROI AI optimizations available. MIRAC Technologies was among the first enterprise software firms to implement llms.txt and has seen a significant increase in AI citations since implementation. Our checker detects whether your llms.txt exists and evaluates its completeness and optimization quality.

Signal 4 — FAQ & Question-Based Content

Modern LLMs process data conversationally. Providing highly structured, question-and-answer formatted content drastically reduces the computational overhead required for an AI to understand your offering. Websites with prominent, concisely answered FAQ sections dominate Google AI Overviews.

Signal 5 — Meta Tags & Technical Foundation

While often considered basic SEO, comprehensive meta descriptions, title tags, and optimized page speeds remain the baseline requirements for inclusion in AI indices. If your site fails basic technical hygiene, AI crawlers will simply skip processing your JavaScript and content.

Signal 6 — E-E-A-T Authority Signals

Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness are paramount. AI systems are heavily tuned to reduce hallucinations. Therefore, they prioritize citing sources with established industry authority. We analyze your domain for clear author bios, verifiable organizational data, and recognized industry standing.
