The Shift from Links to Entities

Google AI Overviews (AIO) do not rank pages based purely on PageRank. They synthesize answers by selecting the most authoritative entities related to the query. E-E-A-T is no longer a guideline; it is a strict technical filter applied by the Gemini models before synthesis begins.

Structural Prerequisites

Data from over 1,000 sites analyzed by the MIRAC AI Readability Checker reveals that sites lacking Organization and FAQPage schemas are almost universally excluded from AIO results. The models require this structural scaffolding to verify the factual integrity of the text.

Passage Optimization

The sweet spot for AIO citation is a passage block of exactly 40 to 60 words, placed immediately following a clear H2 or H3 question header. This formatting minimizes the computational cost for Google to extract and verify the answer, drastically increasing the likelihood of citation.
