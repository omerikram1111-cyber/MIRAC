Deep Dive Analysis

Gemini respects the 'Google-Extended' user-agent in robots.txt. Crucially, Gemini does not just read text; it maps entities using Google's Knowledge Graph. If your website lacks Organization schema, Gemini has a harder time understanding your business context. Furthermore, Gemini favors content broken into short, highly relevant passage blocks. Our AI Readability Checker specifically tests for Google-Extended permissions and the presence of the structured schemas Gemini relies on to synthesize its highly visible summary answers.

Implementation Strategy

Optimizing for Gemini requires a dual approach: unblocking Google-Extended and establishing entity authority via Organization schema. Gemini synthesizes data, so factual density and clear hierarchical headings are mandatory.
