The Challenge

When we first spoke to this client, they were running 6 separate mine sites with zero visibility between them. Paper-based inventory tracking meant management was flying blind with monthly Excel reports. Procurement was based on guesswork, material was delivered to wrong sites, and there was no way to detect theft or waste.

"Our operations manager was spending 3 days every month just consolidating data from 6 sites into one Excel file — and it was still wrong half the time." — Operations Director

Implementing an ERP in a mining context presents unique environmental and operational challenges. Connectivity at remote sites in Punjab is notoriously intermittent, meaning the system architecture required robust offline-sync capabilities. The sheer volume of material being moved daily meant that traditional manual logging was not just inefficient, but fundamentally incapable of providing the data velocity required for modern supply chain management. By deploying ruggedized mobile devices equipped with offline-first React Native applications, we empowered field operators to log data at the source. This single intervention eliminated the multi-day latency previously associated with paper trails.

Furthermore, the integration of automated weighbridge APIs directly into the PostgreSQL database removed human intervention from the material receipt process. Discrepancies between supplier invoices and actual delivered tonnage were immediately flagged. The resulting financial reclamation fundamentally shifted the unit economics of the mining operation.

The Solution

We built a custom ERP featuring a 6-site real-time dashboard and a mobile app (iOS + Android) for field workers. Every material movement now requires barcode scanning. Procurement approvals are automated, and AI anomaly detection flags unusual inventory patterns instantly. A dedicated supplier portal confirms deliveries.

Implementation followed a rigorous 16-week agile process, culminating in a successful single-site pilot before full rollout.

The Results

Month 1: First anomaly detected (PKR 2.3M discrepancy flagged), revealing systematic theft. Procurement approval dropped from 3 days to 4 hours.

Month 3: Material waste dropped by 67%. Inventory accuracy hit 94%. Reporting time shrank from 3 days to 45 minutes.

Year 1: PKR 180M in documented savings, yielding a 340% ROI. 100% staff adoption was achieved thanks to the intuitive mobile app.

The technical deployment strategy was meticulously phased to minimize operational disruption. We began with a shadowed parallel run at a single, high-volume site. This allowed the machine learning anomaly detection models to train on real-world transaction flows before being activated in a blocking capacity. Once the baseline operational models were established, rolling out the system to the remaining five sites was executed with zero downtime. The management dashboard now aggregates data across all sites in real-time, providing executive leadership with predictive inventory alerts, supplier performance metrics, and granular cost-per-ton analysis. The transition from reactive management to predictive oversight has redefined their operational capability.

"The system paid for itself in 6 weeks. We found more in the first month than the entire cost of the project." — Operations Director, Mining Company, Punjab
