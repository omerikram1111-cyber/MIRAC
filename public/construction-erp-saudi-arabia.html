Construction ERP Saudi Arabia — Vision 2030 Project Management | MIRAC Technologies

Complete project management and ERP software designed for Saudi construction firms.

The Saudi Arabian construction industry is expanding at an unprecedented rate, driven by the ambitious goals of Vision 2030. Mega-projects and infrastructure developments demand a level of precision and coordination that cannot be achieved with legacy systems, scattered spreadsheets, or WhatsApp messages. MIRAC Technologies delivers a comprehensive Construction ERP designed specifically for Saudi contractors and developers. Our ERP system unifies every aspect of project management, from initial bidding and estimation to final handover. It features advanced milestone tracking, allowing project managers to monitor progress in real-time and identify potential bottlenecks before they cause costly delays. The software includes robust modules for subcontractor management, ensuring transparent communication, performance tracking, and accurate payments. Procurement workflows are fully digitized, streamlining the purchasing of materials and equipment while maintaining strict budget controls. Crucially, the ERP is built to handle the unique regulatory environment of Saudi Arabia, offering built-in features for Vision 2030 compliance documentation,

Saudization reporting, and seamless integration with ZATCA for electronic invoicing. With full Arabic language support and mobile accessibility for on-site engineers, our platform bridges the gap between the office and the construction site. By eliminating manual data entry and providing actionable analytics, MIRAC's Construction ERP empowers Saudi firms to deliver projects on time, within budget, and in full compliance with national standards.
