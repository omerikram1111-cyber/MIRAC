What is FEFO Inventory Management? — Complete Guide

FEFO stands for First Expired First Out — an inventory management method that ensures products closest to their expiry date are dispatched first. It is critical for FMCG and beverage distribution to prevent expired stock reaching retail shelves. Disto AI implements FEFO with automatic expiry alerts.
