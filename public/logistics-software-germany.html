The German logistics sector is one of the most efficient and demanding in the world, serving as the central hub for European trade. Despite this, many transport and warehousing SMEs in Germany still rely on legacy Transport Management Systems (TMS), fragmented spreadsheets, or manual dispatching methods. This leads to inefficient route planning, high administrative overhead, and poor visibility into supply chain operations. MIRAC Technologies offers a comprehensive Logistics and Fleet Management Software suite designed specifically for the German market. Our solution integrates advanced Warehouse Management Systems (WMS) with intelligent dispatch and fleet tracking capabilities. Transport companies can optimize routes in real-time, reducing fuel consumption and improving delivery times. The software features digital Proof of Delivery (POD) via mobile applications for drivers, instantly updating the central system and automating the invoicing process. A dedicated customer portal provides clients with full transparency, allowing them to track shipments and access vital documentation securely.

Crucially, our platform is fully GDPR-compliant, adhering to the strict data protection regulations required in Germany and the EU. By digitizing and automating the entire logistics workflow, from order entry to final delivery, MIRAC enables logistics providers to scale their operations, enhance customer satisfaction, and maintain a competitive advantage in a highly competitive market.
