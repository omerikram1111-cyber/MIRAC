Manufacturing ERP Germany — Production Planning & Industry 4.0 | MIRAC Technologies

Empower your manufacturing operations with Industry 4.0 ready ERP designed for the German Mittelstand.

The German Mittelstand is renowned globally for its engineering excellence and high-quality manufacturing. However, to maintain this competitive edge in a rapidly evolving global market, these enterprises must embrace digital transformation. Many manufacturing SMEs still manage complex production schedules, quality control, and supply chains using disjointed software or manual processes. MIRAC Technologies provides a sophisticated Manufacturing ERP customized specifically for the German market. Our solution is built from the ground up to support Industry 4.0 initiatives, facilitating seamless connectivity between the shop floor and the top floor. The ERP delivers advanced production planning capabilities, allowing manufacturers to optimize machine utilization, manage materials effectively, and reduce lead times. It features robust digital quality control modules that ensure every product meets stringent standards while automatically generating necessary compliance documentation. Furthermore, comprehensive supply chain tracking offers end-to-end visibility, mitigating risks associated with component shortages or logistical delays. The platform is designed to be

highly secure and fully GDPR-compliant, protecting sensitive intellectual property and operational data. By centralizing operations into a single, intelligent system, MIRAC's Manufacturing ERP enables German SMEs to increase productivity, lower operational costs, and adapt agilely to changing market demands, securing their position as leaders in innovation.
