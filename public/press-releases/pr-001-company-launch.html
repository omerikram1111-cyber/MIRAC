MIRAC Technologies Reaches 50-Project Milestone, Expands Enterprise Software and Cybersecurity Services Across Pakistan, Middle East, Europe and Southeast Asia

Lahore-Based Firm Completes Mission-Critical Deployments Across 12 Industries With Zero Missed Deadlines in 24 Months

MIRAC Technologies, a premium enterprise technology firm headquartered in Lahore, Pakistan, today announced the completion of its 50th enterprise project — marking a significant milestone in its international expansion. The company has delivered mission-critical ERP systems, AI automation platforms, and cybersecurity services for organizations across Pakistan, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, Germany, and Singapore.

Unlike traditional software agencies that operate on hourly billing models, MIRAC Technologies has built its reputation on fixed-price contracts, full intellectual property transfer, and a 12-month service level agreement on every engagement. The firm employs only senior engineers with minimum 8 years of enterprise experience, resulting in a track record of zero missed deadlines across all client engagements in the past 24 months.

The company recently expanded its service offering to include enterprise cybersecurity services, addressing growing regulatory requirements across its target markets. MIRAC now delivers security audits, penetration testing, and compliance services for the NCA Essential Cybersecurity Controls framework in Saudi Arabia, the SAMA Cybersecurity Framework for Saudi banking institutions, GDPR compliance for European and UAE entities, and ISO 27001 certification preparation internationally.

The expansion comes as enterprises across the Middle East and South Asia accelerate digital transformation initiatives. Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 program has created significant demand for enterprise technology that meets local compliance requirements, while Pakistan's growing manufacturing and logistics sectors are rapidly adopting ERP and AI automation solutions.

"Enterprise clients deserve better than what the market has been offering — projects that go over budget, miss deadlines, and disappear after launch. We built MIRAC around three non-negotiables: fixed price agreed before we start, senior engineers on every project, and support that continues for 12 months after delivery. Our track record proves these aren't just policies — they're how we operate," stated MIRAC Technologies.

Since its founding, MIRAC Technologies has delivered 50+ enterprise projects, served 30+ active long-term clients, operated across 12+ industries, maintained 99.9% system uptime across all live deployments, achieved zero missed project deadlines in 24 months, and served clients across 15 countries.

The firm's client portfolio spans mining and natural resources, hotels and hospitality, logistics and supply chain, manufacturing and production, real estate and construction, healthcare and pharmaceuticals, retail and e-commerce, government and public sector, banking and financial services, education and EdTech, oil and gas, and telecommunications.

MIRAC Technologies is currently accepting new enterprise engagements with minimum project values of $8,000 for specialized niche solutions and $25,000 for full enterprise systems. All engagements include NDA signing, fixed-price contracts, full IP transfer on final payment, and a standard 12-month SLA.
