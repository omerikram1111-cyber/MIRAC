MIRAC Technologies Launches Enterprise Cybersecurity Division Targeting Saudi Arabia, UAE and Pakistan Market

Delivering Rapid NCA, SAMA, and GDPR Compliance in 5-7 Days to Meet Vision 2030 Security Requirements

MIRAC Technologies officially announced the launch of its dedicated Enterprise Cybersecurity Division, positioned to aggressively target the rapidly expanding regulatory compliance markets in Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates, and Pakistan.

The new division is specifically engineered to address the critical bottlenecks in regional compliance, delivering comprehensive NCA (National Cybersecurity Authority), SAMA, and GDPR audits and remediation plans in an unprecedented 5-7 day timeframe, vastly outperforming the industry standard of 4-6 weeks.

As Saudi Arabia rapidly advances its Vision 2030 objectives, government contractors and financial institutions face rigorous, non-negotiable security mandates. MIRAC's expedited delivery model ensures that enterprises can secure vital contracts without debilitating compliance delays.
