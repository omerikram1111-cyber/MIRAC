MIRAC Technologies Launches First Free AI Readability Checker

LAHORE, Pakistan — MIRAC Technologies today announced the launch of the world's first free AI Readability Checker, a powerful diagnostic tool that analyzes websites across six critical technical signals to determine their visibility to leading artificial intelligence systems, including ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, and Apple Intelligence.

The tool, available immediately and completely free of charge at miractechnologies.com/ai-readability-checker, performs a comprehensive analysis in approximately 30 seconds. It evaluates a domain's robots.txt AI crawler permissions, JSON-LD structured schema markup, llms.txt presence, FAQ content formatting, meta tag completeness, and foundational E-E-A-T authority signals. The result is a definitive overall AI readability score ranging from 0 to 100.

"We are witnessing a fundamental paradigm shift in digital discovery. Currently, 85% of business websites are essentially invisible to AI search systems," said a spokesperson for MIRAC Technologies. "Companies have invested heavily in traditional SEO methodologies, but they have not adapted their technical architecture for the new reality where ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity handle a rapidly growing share of all research and discovery queries. We built this tool to help businesses understand exactly where they stand and provide them with the actionable insights needed to evolve."

Early Analysis Reveals Startling Vulnerabilities

Prior to its public launch, MIRAC Technologies utilized the tool to analyze over 1,000 corporate websites across various industries. The findings highlight a severe lack of preparation for the AI era:

  • Average Score: The average AI readability score across all industries was an alarming 35/100.
  • Critical Failure Rate: 85% of analyzed sites scored below 50/100, indicating severe visibility issues.
  • llms.txt Adoption: Only a microscopic 3% of enterprise sites have successfully implemented an llms.txt file.
  • Accidental Blocking: 67% of sites are currently blocking at least one major AI crawler via outdated robots.txt configurations.
  • Schema Advantage: Sites utilizing comprehensive FAQPage schema scored, on average, 40 points higher than those without.

The MIRAC AI Readability Checker not only identifies these deficiencies but provides a clear, step-by-step roadmap for remediation. By empowering organizations to optimize their Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) profiles, MIRAC Technologies is ensuring that businesses do not lose their hard-earned market share to more technically agile competitors.
