AI-Powered Recruitment Agency Software — CV Screening & CRM | MIRAC Technologies

Transform your recruitment agency with AI-powered CV screening and complete CRM automation.

Recruitment agencies face an overwhelming volume of applications, slow screening processes, and fragmented communication across emails, phone calls, and messaging apps. Identifying top talent quickly is critical to satisfying clients and closing placements. MIRAC Technologies has engineered an AI-Powered Recruitment Agency Software tailored to the needs of agencies in the UK, Canada, UAE, and Australia. Our platform fundamentally transforms the hiring process by introducing advanced AI CV screening. The system automatically parses, analyzes, and ranks hundreds of resumes against specific job requirements in seconds, significantly reducing manual effort and eliminating human bias. This intelligent automation ensures that recruiters spend their time engaging with the best candidates rather than sifting through unqualified applications. The software includes a comprehensive Candidate CRM that tracks every interaction, manages the interview pipeline, and automates interview scheduling. Additionally, it features seamless WhatsApp integration, enabling automated candidate outreach, appointment reminders, and instant follow-ups on the candidate's preferred

platform. A dedicated client portal allows employers to review shortlisted profiles, provide feedback, and track the progress of their vacancies in real-time. By streamlining workflows and accelerating the placement cycle, MIRAC's recruitment software empowers agencies to improve their service delivery, reduce time-to-hire, and significantly increase their operational profitability.
