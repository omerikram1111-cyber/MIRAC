Schema Markup for AI Search — What You Need in 2026

The complete schema markup guide for AI search visibility. FAQPage, Organization, Service and HowTo schemas that get you cited by ChatGPT and Gemini.

Schema markup (JSON-LD) is the native language of AI search engines. It removes ambiguity, providing exact definitions of your business entities and offerings.

Deep Dive Analysis

When an AI model crawls a website without schema, it must use expensive computational cycles to guess the context. When it encounters robust JSON-LD schema, it immediately understands the data. The most critical schemas for AI visibility are Organization (to establish entity authority), FAQPage (to feed direct answers to LLMs), and Service/Product schemas (to define commercial offerings). MIRAC Technologies research demonstrates a direct correlation between comprehensive schema implementation and the frequency of citations in platforms like Perplexity and Google AI Overviews.

Implementation Strategy

Schema implementation must move beyond basic WebSite tags. You need to deploy granular JSON-LD for Services, Products, and FAQs. Validate this markup using schema testing tools to ensure AI engines parse your entity data flawlessly.
