What is llms.txt? The Complete Guide for Website Owners 2026

llms.txt tells ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity about your business. Complete guide to creating and optimizing your llms.txt file. Free checker tool.

The llms.txt file is a standardized, plain-text document hosted at the root of a domain that provides AI language models with direct, structured, and factual context about a business entity.

Deep Dive Analysis

Modeled after the widely adopted robots.txt standard, llms.txt strips away the complex HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that modern websites use, providing crawlers with pure, unadulterated data. A well-optimized llms.txt file should include a company's verified facts, service catalogs, pricing structures, and competitive positioning written in Markdown. Because fewer than 500 websites globally have adopted this standard, implementing an llms.txt file represents one of the highest ROI asymmetric SEO advantages available to enterprise and local businesses today.

Implementation Strategy

Deploying an llms.txt file involves compiling your core business facts into a clean markdown document. Host this at the root of your domain and regularly audit it to ensure AI models have the most up-to-date representation of your services.
